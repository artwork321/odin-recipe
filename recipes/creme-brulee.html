Creme Brulee

Description

Creme Brulee is so creamy and melt in your mouth. Today, I will bring you the recipe from sous-chef-jason.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, divided

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F.
  2. Whisk together cream and sugar. Cook in the microwave; whisk to dissove sugar with egg yolks and vanilla.
  3. Pour it into ramekins set and put them all on a roasting pan. Pour hot water into the pan.
  4. Bake in the oven about 50 minutes.
  5. Chill ramekins in the fridge.
  6. Sprinkle sugar evenly over the top and melt it until brown.
