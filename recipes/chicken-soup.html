Chicken soup

photo from allrecipes

Description

Chicken soup is so soft, mushy and smooth that can warm your heart. It is an ideal dish for increasing your appetite. Today, I will bring you the recipe from The Allrecipes Community

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 4 carrots
  • 4 stalks celery
  • 1 large onion
  • water
  • salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil over medium heat a pot of chicken, carrots, celery and onion.
  2. Remove chicken; chop meat into pieces, and discard skin and bones.
  3. Strain out vegetables, reserving the stock. Chop them and put all into tthe soup pot.
  4. Heat through the soup, season with spicy you want.
