Fish and Chips

photo from thespruceeats

Description

Fish and chips is a traditional British dish. It will make your fish and potato more delicious than KFC Chicken. Today, I will bring you the recipe from Diana Rattray.

Ingredients:

For the Fish

  • 55 grams all-purpose flour
  • 55 grams cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/3 cup dark beer, water
  • 4 thick, white fish fillets

For the Chips

  • 2 pounds potatos
  • 1 liter vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. Combine flour with the cornstack and baking powder. Season with salt and pepper
  2. Whisk, add beer and water and mix then until the mixture is thick and smooth. Rest it in the fridge.
  3. Cut the potatos into pieces; rinse under cold water, boil and drain them.
  4. Season the fish with salt.
  5. Heat the oil. Cook the chips just for 2 minutes.
  6. Toss each fish fillet in the flour and shake off any excess.
  7. Dip into the batter, coating them.
  8. Fry the fillets until the batter is crisp and golder.
  9. Fry the chips.
